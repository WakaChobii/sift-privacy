Privacy Policy for Sift

Effective Date: February 20, 2026
Last Updated: February 20, 2026

Overview

Sift is a food scanning app that helps users identify gluten-containing ingredients in food products. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Camera Access

  • What we access: Your device camera to scan product barcodes
  • How we use it: Barcode scanning only — no photos or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted
  • Stored locally: No camera data is saved

Barcode Data

  • What we collect: Barcode numbers from scanned products
  • How we use it: Sent to OpenFoodFacts API (a free, open-source food database) to retrieve product information (ingredients, allergens, nutritional data)
  • Third-party sharing: Barcode numbers are transmitted to OpenFoodFacts servers to perform product lookups. OpenFoodFacts is a non-profit organization. See their privacy policy at: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

Scan History

  • What we store: A list of your recently scanned products (product name, barcode, scan date, gluten status)
  • Where it's stored: Locally on your device only, using iOS local storage (AsyncStorage)
  • Sharing: Your scan history is never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Retention: Stored indefinitely until you manually delete items or clear app data

Sensitivity Mode Preference

  • What we store: Your chosen sensitivity mode setting (Regular or Cautious)
  • Where it's stored: Locally on your device only
  • Sharing: Never transmitted to any server

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • No user accounts or login credentials
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers or tracking data
  • No analytics or usage statistics
  • No advertising data

How We Use Your Data

  • Camera: To scan barcodes on food products
  • Barcode lookups: To retrieve product ingredient information from OpenFoodFacts
  • Local history: To show you your recent scans for quick reference
  • Preferences: To remember your sensitivity mode setting

Third-Party Services

OpenFoodFacts API

We use the OpenFoodFacts API to retrieve product information. When you scan a barcode, the barcode number is sent to OpenFoodFacts servers. OpenFoodFacts is a free, open-source, non-profit food database.

  • OpenFoodFacts website: https://world.openfoodfacts.org
  • OpenFoodFacts privacy policy: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

We do not use any other third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Data Security

  • All local data (scan history, preferences) is stored securely on your device using iOS standard local storage mechanisms
  • We do not maintain any servers or databases containing user data
  • Barcode transmissions to OpenFoodFacts use HTTPS encryption

Your Rights

Access Your Data

All your data is stored locally on your device. You can view your scan history in the app's History screen.

Delete Your Data

  • Delete individual scans: Swipe left on any item in the History screen
  • Delete all data: Go to iOS Settings → Sift → Delete App (or offload app) to remove all local data

Opt-Out

Since we do not collect personal data or use tracking/analytics, there is no opt-out required.

Children's Privacy

Sift does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not require registration and does not collect identifiable information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Medical Disclaimer

Sift is not a medical device. This app provides ingredient information only and is not a substitute for medical advice. Always verify ingredients on product packaging and consult your healthcare provider regarding dietary restrictions and medical conditions.
